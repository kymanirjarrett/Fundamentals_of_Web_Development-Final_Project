Who is Gxlden


Hi. My name is Kymani, but I go by the name Golden. I'm a streamer / content creator from Jamaica, and I have a passion for playing video games and editing. My name originated from Xbox 360, and was shortened over time to be easier to say. My main game at the moment is Call of Duty, however I play a variety of games ranging from FPS, open-world, campaign and sandbox genres.

I’ve played games such as Minecraft, Grand Theft Auto 5, Red Dead Redemption 2, Days Gone, Uncharted, and countless others. Currently, I’m a part of an Esports organization originating from Jamaica known as Prime Valor, which allows me to showcase my gaming and editing skills to the entire caribbean Call of Duty community.
