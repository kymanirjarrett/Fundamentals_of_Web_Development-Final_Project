Rates and Contracts


My clients have varying rates dependent on the volume of videos that they need to be edited. I generally try to keep videos that will be posted on tiktok under 1 minute in order to avoid it being muted.

Videos to be posted on instagram are usually between 60 seconds and 90 seconds, however, if the video will not include any copyright music, then videos can be up to five minutes. Pricing for short videos (less than five minutes) are only determined by the number of videos produced, and have a fixed rate regardless of the length of the video. Youtube videos however, have varying rates dependent on the length of the video.
